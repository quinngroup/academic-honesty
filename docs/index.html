Academic Honesty

A podcast for academics by academics about academia: the ups and downs of the academy and the moments in between conference deadlines.

Episode 1: What is "productivity"?

In the podcast's inaugural episode, the full panel discusses productivity in academia: what it is, what it should be, and how we might be able to bridge the two. There's a lot of discussion around how activities that are dismissed by academic culture as "unproductive" actually help our productivity in the long run, and BONUS: don't kill us (or, at least, don't kill us as quickly).
